## About this home

Welcome to your dream home in the highly sought-after Fox Point community! Step into the grand two-story entryway, flooded with natural light, setting the tone for the elegance and comfort throughout. This spacious residence features five bedrooms and three and a half baths, offering plenty of room for family and guests. The kitchen is a chef's delight-perfect for hosting large gatherings-and seamlessly flows into the inviting great room with a cozy fireplace. Just off the kitchen, enjoy the sun-drenched porch overlooking the sprawling backyard and tranquil creek, ideal for morning coffee or evening relaxation. The vaulted ceilings in the primary bedroom allow relaxation and space, along with a walk in closet and ensuite bath. Three bedrooms and a full bath complete the second story. The finished basement is an entertainer's dream, offering ample space for recreation and relaxation. Outside, the incredible yard provides both beauty and privacy, and is just a short walk to the neighborhood pool, playground, and sports courts. This is more than a home-it's a lifestyle.
